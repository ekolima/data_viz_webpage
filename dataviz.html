This visualization is part of a very powerful story describing how homeless people in the US are moving in diffrent cities. For sure, the topic is quite interesting to explore, as it allows us to understand existing perceptions and how local authorities act to alleviate homelessness. It's not only the social aspect of the topic that makes this story interesting, but the fact that it's a whole story shown through visualizations along with the "traditional" text.

The idea behind the plot is to unveil insights about the destinations homeless people are headed to when they are provided with tickets in order to relocate. Firstly, the flows are vizualised in the left part of the plot, while the destinations are depicted in the right, aiming to provide information about the income difference between the destination and origin cities. What I like the most about the vizualition are the colours that easily allow to understand that the majority of people are moving to lower income cities and the ability of the plot to convey multiple insights at once.
