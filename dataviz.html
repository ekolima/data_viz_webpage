This visualization is part of a very powerful story describing how homeless people in the US are moved in diffrent cities. For sure, the topic is quite interesting to explore, as it allows us to understand the dynamics is society and how local authorities act to alleviate homelessness. It's not only the social aspect of the topic that makes this story interesting, but the fact that it's a whole story shown through visualizations along with the "traditional" text.

The idea behind the plot is to unveil the truth about the destinations homeless people are headed to when authorities provide them with tickets in order to relocate. Firstly, the flows are vizualised where of people are vizualised and each flow is connected
